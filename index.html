Week 1 (Intro to HTML)

The FED Journey begins here... the Journey to CONQUER FED!!

Today, i met the captain of my FED Journey. Mr Henry, my FED teacher! The first thing Mr Henry taught me was about the various HTML tags such as <p>...</p>, <h1>...</h1>, <ul>...</ul>, <ol>...</ol> and <li>...</li>. Afterwards, I learnt how we can group elemetns together using containers which are created by using the <div>...</div> or <span>...</span> tags. Moving to the theoretical aspect of HTML, I grasped the concept of block-level and inline elemetns.

After a short 5 mins break, we learnt about how there are 3 core pillars of a website, which is HTML, the bones, CSS, the skin and HTML, the brain of a website. Initially, i was confused as i didnt understand what it meant. However, after Mr Henry elaborated further, it all started making sense to me! After learning everything, I put 101% of my brainpower together to write my FIRST Hello World in HTML :D

Great, now I can proudly call myself a front-end devloper!

After this, Mr Henry showed us examples of good and bad websites. We were then tasked to form groups of 4s with our classmates to create presentation slides on what websites we used frequently, why these websites are good. This was a pretty easy task and my group completed it within 30 minutes!

Moving on, I finally faced the first obstacle in my venture to conquer FED. I had to create a "How to make chocolate chip recepie" website using HTML. Without wasting any time, I jumped stright into it and start working away. Soon, I was done with my website. However the recepie website looked like a malfunctioning monotone website created in the 90s. It was unacceptable and I was disappointed at my own work... So when I reached home, I decided to further study what CSS was about to save Mr Henry from seeing my terrible work.

After hours of studying, I learnt how CSS could completely transform your plain vanilla website into a dark chocolate, double chocolate chip with strawberry topping website! I then applied what I had learnt into my recepie website and it was a million times better!

With that, I came to the end of my first venture to conquer FED!! All in all, it was rather smooth sailing and I made it out alive!
